ESKID

Universal Script for Roblox games.

Our Script has a simple and easy-to-use interface, as well as optimization to run on any Executor, even the free ones. Focusing on FPS and PVP games, it brings Aimbot and Visuals with easy setup. You can save your configuration, change the Menu colors and save as many changes as you want, you can also put some config to load as default on the next use.

Features

Features

Aimbot

Aim at the enemy easily, hit more shots and eliminate faster, You can also create a legit config, with less fov and higher smooth, or you can rage with 0 smooth and max fov.

Visuals

See players through walls, get their information like Name, Distance, Health and build your strategy. You also have functions like seeing if there is someone out of your fov, limiting the draw distance and more.

Configs & Themes

You can modify the colors as you like or use a built-in theme. As well as themes, the settings you have made in Aimbot and Visuals can be saved and set as defaults to be automatically loaded in the next use.
